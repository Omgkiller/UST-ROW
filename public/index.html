Who are we?

The Rowing Club, HKUSTSU was established in 1993 by a small team of 6 passionate boys. Our club and team was built from nothing - no boats or oars nor any rowing experience. Through the accumulation of knowledge from our predecessors and coaching from our alumni rowers, we grew stronger and faster.

Since then, we have grown to a team of 37 rowers (17 boys and 20 girls in 2021). We participate in both scull and sweep, flat-water and costal rowing events. In the most anticipated event of the year - the 2021 Jackie Chan Challenge Cup Hong Kong Universities Rowing Championships, we had won Overall 1st Runner Up and Gold in various scull and sweep events.
